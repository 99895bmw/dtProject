How to Describe Your Issue for Email Support:

To help us understand your situation clearly and provide the best possible advice, please include the following details in your email:

  • Your Name and Location (District, State): This helps us understand your local context.
  • Crop(s) Affected: Specify which crop(s) you are having trouble with.
  • Detailed Description of the Problem:
    • What symptoms are you observing (e.g., on leaves, stems, roots, fruits)?
    • When did you first notice the problem?
    • How much of your crop area is affected?
    • Are neighboring farms experiencing similar issues?
  • Recent Farming Practices:
    • What fertilizers or pesticides have you applied recently? (Name, dosage, date)
    • What are your irrigation practices?
    • Any recent weather events (e.g., heavy rain, drought, sudden temperature change)?
  • Photos (if possible): Clear photos of the affected plants or soil can be very helpful. Please attach them to your email.

Compose an email with these details and send it to our support address.
