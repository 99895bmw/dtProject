Understanding Agricultural Chemicals & Promoting Safety

Chemical inputs like fertilizers and pesticides can significantly boost crop yields, but their misuse or overuse can lead to serious environmental and health problems. It's crucial to use them responsibly.

Fertilizers: Use Wisely

Fertilizers provide essential nutrients to plants. However, balanced fertilization based on soil testing is key. Overuse of nitrogenous fertilizers can contaminate groundwater and increase greenhouse gas emissions. Promoting organic manures, compost, and biofertilizers can reduce reliance on chemical fertilizers and improve soil health.
